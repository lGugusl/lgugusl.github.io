About Me

hungry for new ways to learn and create with sounds,
currently in the city of Angoulême (France). After I got my BTEC Higher National Diploma (in audiovisual course) with sound option, which perfected my skills with audiovisual technical structures and their exploitation, audio recording, live operations, post-production mixing, workflow integration and teamwork, my goal was to pursue a dream and a project that mixed the two greatest passions in my life : Sound and Videogames. Sound has always been a huge part of how I see the world, searching constantly my environnement for new things to hear, learning how to capture and manipulate it, through every day sounds and music equally. Moreover, like with sounds, when it comes to videogames, the more I played and the more I learned about how it was thought and created, the more I wanted to know about, to play with new ideas and to try it for myself. So following some work within the live events industry, I decided to pursue my studies and I entered a vocationnal degree with an audio design option in 2013 to perfect my skills and my experience with sound and its usages, then entered this year the ENJMIN in Angoulême (France) with a sound option. I soon discovered that learning how to think, plan and manipulate audio integration through a videogame project was going to be my main goal for this formation. My everyday life is mainly dedicated to training with sound and its integration within interactive media, while discovering and sharing as many games, movies, books, music and lectures as I can.
